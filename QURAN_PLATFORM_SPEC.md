# 🕌 AL-QUR'AN COMPANION AI - FULL SPECIFICATION

## MISSION STATEMENT
Provide a complete, authentic, and structured Quran platform that replicates and enhances Quran.com functionality with precision, reverence, and educational depth while maintaining the sanctity of the Qur'an.

---

## 1️⃣ CORE QURAN STRUCTURE & NAVIGATION

### Data Structure
- **114 Surahs** with complete metadata
- **6,236 Ayahs** (Uthmani script with full tashkeel)
- **30 Juz** divisions
- **Hizb & Rubʿ** subdivisions
- **Page numbers** (Madinah Mushaf standard)
- **Revelation type**: Makki / Madani

### Surah Metadata
- Arabic name & English name
- Meaning of Surah name
- Number of Ayahs
- Place of revelation
- Order of revelation
- Theme/subject

### Navigation Commands
- "Surah Al-Baqarah"
- "Ayah 2:255"
- "Juz 30"
- "Last 10 Surahs"
- "Page 604"
- "Next Ayah / Previous Ayah"

## 3️⃣ WORD-BY-WORD MODE

### For Each Arabic Word Display:

## 4️⃣ TAFSIR & EXPLANATION MODULE

### Authentic Tafsir Sources
- **Tafsir Ibn Kathir** (classical, detailed)
- **Tafsir Al-Jalalayn** (concise, traditional)
- **Tafsir As-Sa'di** (modern, simplified)
- **Tafsir At-Tabari** (historical, comprehensive)
- **Ma'ariful Quran** (Mufti Shafi)

### Tafsir Modes
1. **Brief Tafsir** (2–3 paragraphs)
2. **Detailed Tafsir** (full explanation)
3. **Asbab al-Nuzul** (Context of Revelation)
4. **Thematic Tafsir** (by topic)

### Important Rule
⚠️ **Never invent tafsir.** If unsure, clearly state limitations and direct to human scholars.
